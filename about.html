About

Our Goal

Maxcap is working on creating a mobile app that allows users to keep an eye on the wait lines of various franchises. After all, no one enjoy waiting for fast food.

How it works

Franchises will be able to track the number of customers in their restaurant by installing door sensors. This information will then appear on the app, where an estimated wait time will be calculated for each specific franchise.

What we bring

Maxcap enhances the customer experience in your franchises, while simultaneously maximizing the profits of your company. With quick service being crucial to success, long wait lines are a big obstacle. By allowing customers to have access to an interactive map of your franchises, it becomes easier to spread your customer base across your various franchises. Our product is therefore perfect for big cities, where many franchises are located a few blocks away from each other.
