Our Method

Hardware Installation

Cutting edge laser sensors are installed at the entrances, that accurately determine the amount of people entering and leaving the store.

Data Storage

This data is uploaded and stored in our secure databases. The information is then processed and returns very accurately approximated wait times.

Live Updates

The wait times are then updated in real-time on the user interface of the franchise's app. This information is then accessible by the user in a simple format.
